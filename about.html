About CMIONIX

CMIONIX is an independent applied engineering and technical consulting initiative focused on embedded systems, control engineering, digital signal processing, and electronic hardware development. The work is aligned with ongoing doctoral research in electrical and computer engineering and centers on real-time systems, deterministic control implementation, and embedded signal processing. CMIONIX is open to non-defense consulting engagements, research collaborations, and early-stage product development support.
